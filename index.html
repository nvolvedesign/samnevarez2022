Why the Mayan pyramid?

The photo of the pyramid above was taken when I visted Chichén Itzá in Mexico's Yucatán. During my visit, I was very intrigued with Mayan architecture. The Mayans were known for incorporating mathematics and spatial awareness within their designs. The shadows on these structures helped the Mayans determine the proper time to cultivate crops and used these structures as centeral points to gather. Today, the ruins demonstrate what it means to design methodically and with intention. This experience inspired me to design and code my own porfolio to resemble an ancient time. I created my own icons and animated background effects of the pyrimid to personalize my site.
