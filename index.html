Why the Mayan pyramid?

After visiting Chichén Itzá in Mexico's Yucatán, I was intrigued with Mayan architecture. The Mayans were known for incorporating mathematics and spatial awareness within their architecture. The shadows on these structures helped the Mayans determine the proper time to cultivate crops. Today, the ruins demonstrate what it means to design methodically and with intention.
